Built for Large Property owners
Our platform is built by property owner for property owners. It streamlines the rental process, offering automated tools to manage properties efficiently from anywhere in the world.
Inventory of Properties
Manage your entire portfolio with GIS-based inventory tracking for land, buildings, and individual units. Visualize property locations on a map, organize assets by type, and track detailed information for each plot, building, and flat.
Secured Rent Collection
Secured Rental Payments powered by RMA Payment Gateway. - Keep track of all your payments in one place for easy bookkeeping. Get notified as soon as your tenant pays, or if their payment is past due.
Lease Agreements
Draft and customize lease agreements in accordance with the Tenancy Act 2015. Set up Move and Move out checklist to ensure hassle-free onboarding and outboarding of tenants.
SMS and Reminders
Set up automatic late fees and enable autopay for tenants. Receive email reminders for timely payments and notifications for overdue rent. SMS reminders to all your tenants
Maintenance & Damage Items
Allow tenants to quickly report maintenance issues and property damage.Managers can track the status of each request and add resolution of status.
